We are currently building Shared Cinema as an open source project that has the potential to be used by a variety of audiences.

We envision this video jukebox will transform many forms of group gatherings, from a bus stop in a busy downtown to a film festival trailer screening room, from a neighborhood bar to your own backyard party. Anywhere two or more people are gathered together Shared Cinema brings a group media viewing experience.

We are sure that an open source Shared Cinema will be used and hacked upon in ways we have to yet to image!
